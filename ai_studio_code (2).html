Know Your Surgeon.

Dr Nisanth Puliyath is a urologist specializing in robotic uro-oncology, with expertise in minimally invasive surgery for urological cancers, including prostate, kidney, and bladder malignancies. His practice is rooted in precision, evidence-based medicine, and individualized patient care.

He completed his MBBS from Government Medical College Kozhikode, followed by MS in General Surgery from the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), and subsequently MCh in Urology from Government Medical College Kozhikode.

He further advanced his training in robotic surgery and uro-oncology at Medanta, Gurugram, through the esteemed Vattikuti Fellowship.
